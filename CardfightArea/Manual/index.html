  • Basics

  • How to play this card game?

    Cardfight Area cannot teach you how to play the card game itself. Fortunately, there are a lot of videos explaining exactly that. Here is the official one - it is a good starting point.

  • How do I get a deck to play?

    We have a number of basic decks included with Area, you can find them in "Decks" folder. To open a deck, press "Load deck" on the main screen. To add your own deck, you will have to build it using in-app Deck Editor.

  • Playing Field

  • How to play with a Ride Deck?

    You have to use gray circles under the drop zone to place your Ride Deck cards there. It can be done automatically if you pick cards by CTRL+Clicking on them while building your deck in Deck Editor - but to make it work you have to pick grades 0, 1, 2 and 3.

  • Will opponent see my starting Vanguard?

    They will unless you use a Ride Deck.

  • How to open my deck?

    Press blue magnifying glass icon to open your deck, or you can CTRL+Click the deck as well. Press red magnifying glass icon to open your G-Zone.

  • How can I perform a drive or damage check?

    To drive check, right-click on your deck. Power bonus activates automatically. Same goes for damage check, but you have to be in your End Phase and opponent has to be in their Battle Phase.

  • How to open my soul?

    You have to hold CTRL and click your vanguard.

  • How to open bind/drop/removed from game zone?

    Right-click the zone.

  • How to perform a counter blast/counter charge?

    Right-click on a damage zone card to flip it.

  • How to perform a soul blast/soul charge?

    To perform a soul blast open your soul by CTRL+Clicking your Vanguard. Then, right-click any card to put it into drop zone. To perform a soul charge, find corresponding right-side button and click it. To put cards from other zones into soul, select a card and then click on your Vanguard. When prompted with a question, click "To Soul" button.

  • How to cancel a selection?

    If you want to un-select a card or remove additional power/shield/crit selected, press ESC.

  • How do I put cards into bind face-up from the field?

    Having selected a card, hold shift when clicking on bind. Alternatively, you can flip them while they are there using right-click.

  • How to draw cards from the bottom of my deck?

    Hold shift while you click on your deck.

  • How do I lock my cards?

    Hold CTRL while clicking on a card.

  • How do I delete my Vanguard?

    Hover your mouse cursor over the Vanguard and press DELETE on your keyboard. If you do not have one on your keyboard, Fn+Backspace might work for you.

  • How do I apply power to all units or to a whole row?

    After clicking on a button that gives power, instead of clicking a unit, click on a right-side button that says ALL UNITS or THIS ROW respectively.

  • Can I view history of actions?

    To open action log, hold Q key on your keyboard.

  • Can I view the entire history of actions?

    Not in the game. You have to save it using CTRL+S key combination. Sometimes the log file is being locked from writing to it by Windows, so when an error pops up just press ignore, but the log will not be saved. Try manually deleting log.txt, it might fix your issue.

  • How do I move or remove a marker from one of my circles?

    For accel circle, remove unit on it first, then hover it and press DELETE key. For normal rear-guard circles, first you have to right-click the circle itself. A unit on the circle might block your view, so aim just right next to the unit. This action picks up your marker. If you have more than one type of markers on that circle, it will grab one of each type. You can now click on another circle to move the markers. If you wish to remove marker(s) being held altogether, click on Removed From Game zone. To re-apply a marker you could activate one from a Removed From Game zone.

  • How do I get a marker or a token using a unit's ability?

    While holding Shift, Click on a unit that has such an ability.

  • How do I close Astral Plane?

    After retiring Astral Deity, hold Shift and click the circle.

  • How can I see previous chat messages?

    You can use your mouse scroll inside a chat window. Bear in mind that these are only saved on your side for the duration of your current play session.

  • How do I paste text into chat?

    You can just ctrl+V inside chat. But don't send long messages or the server will think that you are trying to spam.

  • Deck Editor

  • How do I choose another clan?

    Click the plate on top with an arrow that points down.

  • How do I exit the editor without saving my deck?

    You have to load any deck first, then exit. This feature is to prevent players from playing unsaved decks that are too small.

  • I cannot add any cards to the deck! What can I do?

    In the bottom right corner it says how many cards you have already added. To remove cards you do not need, right-click them. To empty the current list altogether you have to click New Deck before you start building a new one.

  • I tried searching for a card and I cannot find it! What could be the problem?

    Search function is case-sensitive, so what you type should exactly match the text on the card, letter-by-letter.

  • How do I cancel the search?

    You have to make a new search and click OK without changing the input. It effectively resets the search. Switching between different filters works as well.

  • Server

  • How do I get back to the main screen?

    Currently, you cannot do that. You will have to restart Cardfight Area.

  • How do I play my favorite format?

    You can go to one of the thematic rooms - to the right from spawn is V-Premium room, and going down from the spawn is Standard (Overdress) room. There is no dedicated room for Premium, but you can find your opponent in a room other than those two.

  • How can I obtain my favorite sleeves?

    Sleeves are currently distributed at random from a certain pool among people standing in the rightmost bottom room, the Ice Castle. One sleeve is being distributed approximately every 45 minutes, two if there are at least 30 people standing in the room. It is possible can get duplicate of the same sleeve.
